a skill.md
plus rules.

Claude Design is a SKILL.md plus a handful of on-demand references. Claude loads it when your request sounds like design — a deck, a landing page, a prototype, a poster, a motion piece.

Once loaded, it rewires Claude's defaults. Fact verification before assumption. Real brand assets before colours and fonts. Three directions before commitment.

three rules.

// RULE_01

verify before assuming.

When a product is named, WebSearch comes first. 10-second search beats 1–2 hours of rework. Priority zero. Non-negotiable.

// RULE_02

gather the real stuff.

Ask → search → download → verify → freeze. Logos, product shots, UI screenshots first. Colours second. Not the other way around.

// RULE_03

three, then commit.

Vague brief? Three directions from ten philosophies across five schools. Preview. User picks. Full commit, no hedging.
