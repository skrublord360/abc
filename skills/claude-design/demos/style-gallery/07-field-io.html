A small file, a very different default.

Claude Design is a SKILL.md plus a handful of on-demand references. Claude loads it when your request sounds like design — a deck, a landing page, a prototype, a poster, a motion explainer.

Once loaded, the skill rewires Claude's instincts. Fact verification before assumption. Real brand assets before colours and fonts. Three directions before committing. Not a bigger model — a different set of defaults.

Three operating principles.

02 / 04 · Priorities
01 · Priority Zero

Verify facts before assuming.

When a product is named, WebSearch comes before design. 10 seconds of search beats 1–2 hours of rework. Non-negotiable.

02 · Core Asset Protocol

Gather the real stuff.

Ask → search → download → verify → freeze. Logos, product shots, UI screenshots are first-class. Colours and fonts are auxiliary.

03 · Direction Advisor

Offer three, then commit.

When the brief is vague, propose three directions from ten philosophies across five schools. Build a preview. Wait for the user.
